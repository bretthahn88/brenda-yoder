> A new school year is almost here! How can you best equip your child, whether starting preschool or college, to be strong and courageous in this world of adversity?

Whether you send your kids to public school, private school, or homeschool, it can be terrifying as a parent. And it doesn't matter how old your kid gets, it's still just as hard. The Bible describes it best when Jesus says to his disciples, "I am sending you out like sheep among wolves." (Matthew 10:16). Yup, that pretty much encompasses how every parent feels sending their child out into the world.

So what are you to do?

In this episode, Brenda gives three helps for parents who are not only sending their child off to school, but eventually out into the world. This episode (and podcast) will hopefully bring encouragement to parents and kids alike to live courageously in the love of Christ Jesus.
