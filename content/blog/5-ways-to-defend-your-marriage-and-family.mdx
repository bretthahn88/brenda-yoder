> ### Learn how to be proactive in defending your marriage and family against the attacks of the enemy.

Has your marriage been impacted by pornography, anger, strife, disconnection, or indifference? How about your kids? Our families are targets for the enemy to take down, divide, and destroy. But don't be discouraged! Satan is defeated and Jesus Christ is the Victor! With that in mind, we must stay actively engaged in battle.

Listen in as Brenda shares five practical ways that you can become a warrior and guardian in your home through the power of Jesus Christ.
