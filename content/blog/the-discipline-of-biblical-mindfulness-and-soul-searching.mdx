> When you hear the word 'mindfulness', do you instantly think of biblical mindfulness or worldly mindfulness?

Worldly Mindfulness originates from the early teachings of Buddha and involves the practice of clearing one's mind, focusing on your breathing, being present in the moment, etc.

Biblical Mindfulness, looked at more deeply in today's episode, is based on Scripture that calls us to develop the discipline of being mindful and still before God. Instead of emptying our minds, we fill it with the truth of God's Word and the fruit of the Holy Spirit.
