We're chatting again with Andrea Miller for the second part of her interview on [overcoming life's big disappointments. ](https://brendayoder.com/overcoming-lifes-big-disappointments-with-andrea-miller/)

In the first part of her story, she shared the heartbreak of permanent infertility, a failed adoption, and the bankruptcy of an adoption agency where they had invested their money. In this episode, Andrea shares the rest of her powerful story of further loss, how God sustained her, and joy through grief and disappointment.

Andrea has a powerful end to her story, and we're excited to share it with you!

Please also pray for Andrea. She is a Charge nurse in a COVID unit and is facing the front lines of our pandemic.
