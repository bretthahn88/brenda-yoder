I want to share a blog post written by a classmate and young woman I had the honor to mentor while I was in graduate school. She and her husband, Kyle, are devoted to full time ministry and have chosen to adopt their first child. Read their story at [The Brenneman Life](http://thebrennemanlife.wordpress.com/2012/06/03/maybe-baby-take-2/?blogsub=confirming#subscribe-blog), and be sure to read their first experience with adoption linked in their blog.

Sometime older generations get discouraged with younger generations. This couple is one example that the light of the Lord is shining bright, and perhaps even deeper than generations past.

*"Do not let anyone look down on you because you are young." I Timothy 4:12a*
