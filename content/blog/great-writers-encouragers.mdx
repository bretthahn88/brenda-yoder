*Several weeks ago, my friend Tracy at [My Daily Walk in His Grace](http://mydailywalkinhisgrace.blogspot.com/) gave me the Versatile Blogger Award. I want to thank Tracy for this honor, and I would like for you to visit her encouraging blog and share in her truths of God's word. I would also like to share this encouragement by extending this award to a few blogs that are my favorite - please visit them! You will be glad you did.*

[](http://brendayoder.files.wordpress.com/2012/07/versatileblogger113-sm11.png?w=150)Please visit;

Cindy at [She Sparkles](http://www.cindybultema.com/) - I recently met Cindy at a conference, and she is a true woman of God!

JJ at [The Landii](http://www.jjlandis.com/) - JJ is a talented writer, a classmate I've reconnected with, and an authentic woman!

Lee at [To Be Like A Butterfly](http://tobelikeabutterfly.blogspot.com/) - she is a dear friend from high school, just started blogging, and is a woman of freedom!

Amelia at [Amelia Rhodes](http://www.ameliarhodes.com/) - I also just met Amelia, and she has a beautiful, humble heart.

Denise at [Refine Me](http://friskychile.blogspot.com/) - she has biblical encouragement every time you visit her site.

Kim at [Approaching the Vintage Years](http://approachingthevintageyears.blogspot.com/) - a good friend full of wisdom and humor, and love for God.

Janette at [Janette's Sage](http://janettessage.blogspot.com/) - Janette is a woman living life authentically with all of her readers!

Lorraine at [The Multidimensional Life](http://lorrainelorrainelorraine.blogspot.com/) - a creative, talented woman bringing joy and creativity to life!

Lidia at [Crown of Glory](http://mla-crownofglory.blogspot.com/) - my spiritual mentor and woman who truly loves the Lord!

Joan at [Beauty in His Grip](http://www.thebeautyinhisgrip.com/) - a real encourager and good biblical insights.

The Unternahrers at [The Best of Times, the Worst of Times](http://unternahrers.blogspot.com/) - dear friends sharing their journey on the mission field in the Dominican Republic.

Jessica at [God is Love](http://jessicasperuvianarchives.blogspot.com/) - another friend whose passion is the mission field in Peru.

Dakota at [A Look at Life from the Deer Stand](http://www.lifefromadeerstand.com/) - a man of God sharing his passion of God and nature.

Amanda at [Raspberry Beige](http://www.raspberrybeige.com/) - a creative friend who has a bright spot in blogging world.

There are many great blogs out there, these are just a few I'd love to highlight today, because they have blessed me!

"*In all my prayers for all of you, I always pray with joy because of your partnership in the gospel from the first day until now..." Philippians 1:4*
