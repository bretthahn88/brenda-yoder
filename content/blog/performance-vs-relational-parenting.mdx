> "Loving your kids through authentic relationship is the greatest way to open their hearts to Christ." Brenda Yoder, Fledge: [Launching Your Kids Without Losing Your Mind. ](https://brendayoder.com/fledge-launching-kids-without-losing-mind/)

Even though parents don't intend to, we send our kids an early message that our child's worth is base on what they do, not who they are. This performance type of parenting is hurtful to kids as they strive to conform to a parent's approval rather for being loved for who they are. In this podcast, Brenda discusses a powerful principle from her book [Fledge: Launching Your Kids Without Losing Your Mind. ](https://brendayoder.com/fledge-launching-kids-without-losing-mind/)
