> Emotions are healthy when expressed in a healthy manner, but they can also reek havoc, too.

We are whole people! Just as important as our physical and mental health is our emotional health. Emotions are healthy when expressed in a healthy manner, but they can also reek havoc, too. In this podcast episode, Brenda discusses the importance of emotional health.
