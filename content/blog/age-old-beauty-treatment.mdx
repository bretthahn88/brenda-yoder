A wrote a post at my other blog, His Story, and wanted to share it here, too. A short one, on God's love story with us. Simple truths: .

From reading His Story with the Israelites, His chosen people, I stand amazed that He always comes back and loves them and provides for them.

Overview:
*The loves them, they rebel and walk away from him. He loves them, they worship other gods, He comes to live with them, they desecrate Him Name. He disciplines them for a time, sends them away, then **Guess What?** He loves them so much He calls them home and loves them again.*
*
*
Wow! I don't need to comment to much on this - just a note of encouragement - if He's that way with a group of people, his chosen ones,

How much more is He like that with Us?

And ladies, don't we need to hear that - every day - every minute of every day?

He loves us, we rebel and walk away, He loves us, we give our worship to other things, He comes to live in us (the Holy Spirit) and we desecrate His Name by our choices. He disciplines us for a time, then .....He loves us so much, calls us home, and loves us again.

If you needed to hear that today, then know You are loved, rest in that, and praise the Living God.

No better aging or beauty treatment than that for the day. You are looking good, wrapped in his love!

"*Neither death nor life, neither angels, not demons, neither the present not the future, not any powers, neither the present nor the future, not any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God that is in Christ Jesus our Lord." Romans 8: 37-39*
