Do you struggle to balance being the good wife when it feels like you have nothing left for your husband after caring for kids and others all day long? It's a common feeling for women with kids. In this episode, Brenda tackles these marriage issues with three powerful principles:

- Simply love your spouse (learn more).
- Work on your own stuff (see what Brenda means by that).
- Be on the same team (yes, you're not against each other).

Much of this podcast is taken from a chapter in Brenda's book, "[Balance, Busyness, and Not Doing It All](https://www.amazon.com/Balance-Busyness-Not-Doing-All/dp/1511966750/)" and from retreats and talks she does on marriage!

Scripture: [1 Corinthians 13: 4-7](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=1+Corinthians+13%3A4-7&version=NIV)
