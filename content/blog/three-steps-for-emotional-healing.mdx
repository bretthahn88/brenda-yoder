"*They begged him to let them touch even the edge of his cloak, and all who touched him were healed.*" Mark 6:56

What do think of when you hear the word emotional healing? Is it something you long for?

When I think of healing, I think of **freedom** from pain.

**Where do you run when needing relief from pain?** For years I ran to an eating disorder, to people and their approval, to my rights and control instead of running to the Healer. **He was standing right there, but I didn't reach out.**

Am I the only one who's stood beside the Healer, plagued with hurt, crying , **"Lord, help me!"** yet didn't get relief? Have you ever **cried out, scraped at your sores and then wondered why God didn't help you?**

They ran throughout the whole region and carried the sick on mats to where ever they heard he was. And wherever he went, into villages, towns or countrysides, they placed the sick in the marketplaces. They begged him to let them even touch the edge of the cloak, and all who touched him were healed. Mark 6:54-56

If you want freedom from pain, grief, bitterness, emotional hurt or broken relationships, **Jesus* i**s capable of healing your pain*. Believe this, then act on it.

The paralytic (Mark 2), the woman bleeding for years (Mark 5) and the people in Mark 6 were healed by Jesus after ***they*** ***came*** to Him,** *believing ****He would heal them****. ***Addictions, faulty thinking, anxiety, over-indulgence, broken relationships, and other non-physical sources of pain just don't disappear with wishful prayer. They require **surrender and action.** [](https://brendayoder.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG_0649ed.jpg)

Would you run to Jesus to touch His hem for healing?** H**ow do you touch Jesus in 2014?

#### **1. Through Prayer **

- **Praise Him** for who He character, His power, and who He is in your life.
- **Thank Him** for the things He has done, is doing, and will do in your life.
- **Confess** any sin against Him or others that's hindering your walk or His work in your life.
- **Ask Him** to reveal and heal ***the source*** of your pain. Don't superficially ask Him to heal you. Ask Him to deal with the garbage that's causing the behavior, the hurt or pain. It's an intimate act, just like touching His cloak is a personal act of faith.

#### **2. Through Faith**

- **Faith is believing what you can't see.** In Jesus' day, the people believed Jesus could heal them if they only touched Him. Do you believe Jesus will heal your pain if you give Him your hurt? Without faith, you limit his healing power in your life (Mark 6:5-6)
- **Faith requires giving up control**. You have control over your emotions and pain whether you admit it or not. When you choose to be healed emotionally, you give up your rights to your pain, bitterness, anger, control over others, fears and worry. The harder you hold on, the harder it is for Jesus to take it from you. Christ is a gentleman. He won't force his way into your life. He will heal when you surrender your control to His.
- **Faith requires being honest.** You can't hide from God. You need to be honest with yourself. Do you really want to be healed? Until you're honest with yourself and God, healing will be stalemated by your unwillingness to let go and let God do His work.

#### **3. Through Action**

- I didn't wake up one day and overcome an eating disorder. **Each healing journey requires a conscious choice **to deal with the behavior, the relationship, the emotions, or the root of your pain.
- Once you decide to heal, **work at it.** This might include formal counseling, coaching, or accountability partners to pray with you. It requires learning about yourself, dealing with the hurt, and releasing the source of pain.
- Daily, you need to **choose Jesus' way** rather than retaliating or holding onto your rights.

Jesus wants us to come to Him, to touch His cloak and heal us. He desires to have the fullness of who He is in the abundant life He has planned for us.

Father will you show us how to draw closer to you? Will you come to us in our pain, giving us the desire to reach out and touch you for healing? Thank you we know you'll meet us each step.
