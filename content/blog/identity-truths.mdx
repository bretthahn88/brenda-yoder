Ladies, a note to you today, where ever today finds you. As women, you are
Blessed (Ephesians 1:3)
Chosen (Ephesians 1:4)
Adopted (Ephesians 1:5)
Accepted (Ephesians 1:6)
Redeemed (Ephesians 1:7)
Forgiven (Ephesians 1:8)

I've been reflecting on identity as women as I prepare for our Mother/Daughter banquet coming up, and the greatest truth we can hear as women is our identity in Christ. The truths above come from Beth Moore's "Believing God" study and is one of the principles from the series.

Far above being wives, mothers, daughters, sisters, friends, professionals, etc., we are women of God, created by Him to reflect Him. We are blessed, chosen, adopted by Him, accepted by Him, redeemed by Him, and forgiven.

Rest in that, today.
