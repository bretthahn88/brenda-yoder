Welcome to Season 4 of the Life Beyond the Picket Fence podcast! We're continuing front porch conversations, those intimate discussions about deep, intrinsic topics.

I'm excited to share a front porch conversation with my friend Andrea Miller! Andrea is a ICU nurse working with COVID patients, but that's not what we'll be talking about. She's also a wife and mom of two gorgeous, spunky preschoolers.

Andrea's story includes adversity and big disappointments, pain, and grief. You'll be encouraged with how she's dealt with life beyond the picket fence image. Some of the issues we'll talk about include:

- broken dreams of motherhood
- infertility
- adoption disappointments
- death
- and more!

Andrea shares practical tips for overcoming disappointments but also how to strengthen your faith when hope seems lost. I'm excited to share her story with you in the next two episodes!
