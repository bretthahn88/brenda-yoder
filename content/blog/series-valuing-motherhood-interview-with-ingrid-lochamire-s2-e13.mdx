The last few weeks have been tough waters to navigate, friends! Please let us know how we can pray for you during the Stay at Home COVID 19 crisis!

We continue with our series Valuing Motherhood during this time to bring encouragement, support, and some normalcy to our days. This final interview with Ingrid Lochamire will give you hope and wisdom in tough times.

For the next two episodes, I'm excited to interview Ingrid Lochamire, a woman whose journey as a woman and mom has powerful lessons from which to learn: abandonment by her mom, broken marriage, being a single mom, blended family, homeschooling, and caring for adult children in crisis.

About Ingrid:

Ingrid Lochamire is an author and speaker who shares "slice of life" stories on[ her blog](http://ingridlochamire.com/) and elsewhere. She and her husband live on a farm in northeast Indiana, where she raised and homeschooled four sons. She fills her empty-nest days with reading, writing, knitting, gardening and ministry to incarcerated women through a weekly writing group. She also leads Bible studies in her church and actively mentors other women as the Lord leads. Connect with Ingrid at [ingridlochamire.com.](http://ingridlochamire.com/)
