> Are you a mom struggling with irritation? Do you feel stressed out and at the breaking point while trying to do it all?

Whether you're a new mom or a seasoned mom, we have all fallen into the trap of trying to be superwoman to everyone around us. We want to be that put together mother who has time to clean, bake, volunteer, be with friends, and enjoy life with well behaved, happy children and a romantic, helpful husband.

But let's all be honest, life as a mom is hard. It's exhausting. And it can be irritating. Sometimes, really REALLY irritating.

In this episode, Brenda shares how she realized she was an angry mom and how she discovered the grace she needed to become healthy again. Subscribe to this podcast so you won't miss Part Two of Help for Grumpy, Irritated Moms coming next Wednesday!
