About Ingrid:

Ingrid Lochamire is an author and speaker who shares "slice of life" stories on[ her blog](http://ingridlochamire.com/) and elsewhere. She and her husband live on a farm in northeast Indiana, where she raised and homeschooled four sons. She fills her empty-nest days with reading, writing, knitting, gardening and ministry to incarcerated women through a weekly writing group. She also leads Bible studies in her church and actively mentors other women as the Lord leads. Connect with Ingrid at [ingridlochamire.com.](http://ingridlochamire.com/)
