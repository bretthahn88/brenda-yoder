Does the title of the book speak to you? Have you ever needed a place to belong? A place of acceptance, authenticity, affirmation, accountability, and action with other women?

> A safe place to be?

I have. When I meet members of the Circle of Friends ministry team at Carol Kent's conference this summer, my internal thoughts were

> Where have you been all my life?

How many times have I needed a safe place to be with other women, especially when walking the most vulnerable and difficult times of life?

Not only am I privileged to partner with them in ministry as one of their devotional writers, but I want to share their ministry with others, and share safe places with others through A Place To Belong*.

Author Lisa Troyer shares with honestly the depression and other personal struggles she has encounters. She shares the life-giving hope authentic relationship with other women provide. She also includes the ability to use this book for a bible study and small group resource for starting a Circle of Friends group.
