> "If there's one phrase that comes to mind when I think of caregivers, it's selfless servants" - Sarah Forgrave, author of Prayers of Hope for Caregivers."

On this podcast episode, I'm interviewing [Sarah Forgrave,](https://www.sarahforgrave.com/) author of [Prayer of Hope for Caregivers](https://www.sarahforgrave.com/books/prayers-of-hope-for-caregivers/) about caregiver's needs. We talk about her role as caregiver and how caregivers are in a unique role that she is breathing life into!

Grab a copy of Sarah's book, along with her first book [Prayers of Hope and Healing](https://www.sarahforgrave.com/books/prayers-for-hope-and-healing/). You'll be refreshed and encouraged by this interview!
