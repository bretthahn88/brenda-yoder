> We are inundated with messages each day that we are not enough. Do you struggle with this?

If you grew up hearing about the Proverbs 31 Woman, you don't need to hear much more. She is on top of her game and puts us all to shame. As we measure ourselves up against our friends, what we see in social media and the entertainment industry, even strangers at the store, we feel that we can never measure up. No matter how hard we try, our efforts to be everything for everybody fail.

But what if we said enough is enough!?

In this episode, Brenda unpacks the truths in Proverbs 31 that will breathe new life into your anxiety wrecked soul and breaks down the lies that we believe that we are not enough. Are you ready?
