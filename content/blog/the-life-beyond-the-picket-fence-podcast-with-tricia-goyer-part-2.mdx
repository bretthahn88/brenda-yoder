> Grab your coffee and enjoy part 2 of the Tricia Goyer interview!

Join Brenda as she interviews Tricia Goyer on her new book The Grumble Free Year (release date 11/05/19) in which Tricia and her husband sought out a solution to their grumbling household. They and their children agree to living grumble free for an entire year. So not only were the children expected to harness their tantrums, attitude, or criticizing; so were the parents. Sounds fun, right?!

In this episode, Tricia speaks to the importance of reevaluating expectations, practicing, and giving yourself (and your kids) grace and space. If we expect our homes to be thankful and gracious, we must first model that behavior. In addition to modeling an attitude of thankfulness, stay persistent even if it is just a little every day.

Take heart, mamas! You are growing as well as your kids, every step in the right direction is a huge accomplishment. Keep going!

##### Meet Tricia Goyer

Tricia Goyer is a busy mom of ten, doting grandma, and wife to John.

A USA Today bestselling author, Tricia has published seventy books and is

a two-time Carol Award winner, as well as a Christy and ECPA Award

Finalist. She won the Retailer'' Best Award in 2015 and has received starred

reviews from Romantic Times and Publishers Weekly. She is also on the

blogging team at TheBetterMom.com and other homeschooling and

Christian sites. Tricia is the founder of Hope Pregnancy Ministries and

currently leads a teen MOPS Group in Little Rock, Arkansas.
