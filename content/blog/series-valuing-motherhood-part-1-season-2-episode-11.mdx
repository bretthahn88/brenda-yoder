> #### Hey Moms, are you having a hard time feeling fulfilled in your role and seeing yourself as valuable?

Whether you're a single mom, a married mom, work outside or inside the home, you have a lot going on! And many times, it seems that all you do goes unnoticed and unappreciated. You may begin to feel like a maid, a daycare, or a failure. Friend, your role as a mother is unlike any other!

### You are doing kingdom work.

Motherhood is near and dear to Brenda' heart and she knows from experience that it comes with both its joys and struggles. If you find yourself discouraged, exhausted, and in need of a friend who will speak truth to you, then this series is for you. Please join us in this 6 part series as Brenda focuses on the beauty of the mothering role despite its hardships.

Maybe grab some tissues!
