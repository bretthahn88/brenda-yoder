> Life as a mom is full of unexpected detours, bumps, and roadblocks. Juggling family and the day to day can be taxing and leave you feeling frustrated and irritable.

We've all been there in one season or another. Overwhelmed by expectations set by ourselves and others. Losing control and lashing out at our family. Dropping into bed feeling like a failure heap. All of this can leave a person believing there is no hope for them and that they are powerless against it.

Good news, mamas, there is hope! Just take it one day at a time and listen as Brenda shares how you can begin your journey towards peace, balance, and joy.

That journey may require that you seek out a trusted friend or professional help. Please know that there is no shame in that. Life, even its struggles, is meant to be shared and carried by each other, not all by ourselves.

Book Recommendations: Boundaries by J. Townsend and H. Cloud, Boundaries for kids/J. Townsend and H. Cloud, Parenting beyond the rules/C. Albers
