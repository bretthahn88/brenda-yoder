> Even as Christians, we can be consumed with the search for our identity, our worth, and a quick fix to our insecurities.

Today, we are surrounded by a wealth of information promising to help us find ourselves, our destinies, and our value. We all struggle to answer these questions and sometimes spend years searching. Am I good enough? Who am I really? Am I valued? Am I loved? What am I supposed to be doing with my gifts?

In this episode, Brenda will share from her personal journey as well as what the Bible has to say on the topic. May it bring peace to your anxious heart, friend.

[Isaiah 43:4,7](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Isaiah+43&version=NIV)
